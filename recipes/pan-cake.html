Pan-Cake

Pan-Cake is a food that is loved by many from kids to adults.

The ingredients and steps of a pancake are so simple that even a person who don't know how to cook can do it just by following the instructions.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • egg
  • butter
  • honey or syrup

Steps

  1. First break the egg to a bowl and then mix in some flour with it.
  2. Heat up the pan while mixing the flour and egg
  3. You can use a shape guider for the pancake but you can just also use a small cup to put some of the mixed flour and egg into the pan.
  4. Wait for it to be color brown and then flip it so that the other side will be also cook
  5. Then add the syrup or honey at the top of your pancake and then also add a butter in it.
