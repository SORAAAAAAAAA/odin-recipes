Carbonara

Carbonara is a great and delicious Italian food that is well known across the globe.

Despite the fact that carbonara is a healthy and low cost food it's recipe is also flexible, because you can use different kinds of ingredients as the carbonara's toppings depending on your choice of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • nestle all-purpose milk
  • 1/2 kg of pasta/Noodles
  • mushroom
  • butter
  • salt and pepper
  • cheese
  • bacon

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package direction.
  2. In a pan, cook the bacon until crispy and set aside.
  3. Using the same pan, add the butter and salute the garlic until light brown. Add sliced mushrooms and all purpose cream.
  4. Arrange a plate for the pasta and then mix the sauce.
