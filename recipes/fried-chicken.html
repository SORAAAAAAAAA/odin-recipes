Fried Chicken

Fried Chicken, this is definetely the food that everybody loves, I mean who can't like fried chicken right? AHhahahha

It may seem that fried chicken is hard to cook but that is definetely not the case just follow these steps and your good to go.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • flour or breading mix
  • pepper
  • some water
  1. put some water on a bowl and then mix it with the flour
  2. dip the chicken or roll it in the bowl
  3. put it in the pan and wait for it to be golden brown
  4. flip the chicken and then your done
